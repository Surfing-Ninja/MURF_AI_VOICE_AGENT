DesiMart Customer Support Procedures & How-To Guides

=== HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER ===

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Browse or Search for Products
   - Use the search bar at the top
   - Or browse by category from the menu
   - Apply filters (price range, brand, features, ratings)

2. Select Your Product
   - Click on the product for details
   - Read specifications, reviews, and ratings
   - Check delivery time for your pincode
   - View available offers and bank discounts

3. Add to Cart
   - Select quantity, color, variant
   - Click "Add to Cart"
   - Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

4. Review Cart
   - Check all items and quantities
   - Apply coupon code if available
   - Check estimated delivery date

5. Proceed to Checkout
   - Sign in or create account (or checkout as guest)
   - Enter delivery address
   - Select delivery speed (Standard/Express/Same-day)

6. Choose Payment Method
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
   - UPI
   - Cash on Delivery
   - EMI option

7. Place Order
   - Review order summary
   - Click "Place Order"
   - You'll receive order confirmation via email and SMS

=== HOW TO TRACK YOUR ORDER ===

Method 1: Website/App
1. Log in to your DesiMart account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Find your order
4. Click "Track Order"
5. View real-time tracking with map

Method 2: Email Link
1. Check your order confirmation email
2. Click on the tracking link
3. No login required

Method 3: SMS
Reply to the order confirmation SMS with "TRACK" to get tracking link

Method 4: Call Support
Call 1800-123-4567 and provide your order number

Tracking Status Meanings:
- Order Confirmed: We've received your order
- Order Processed: Payment confirmed, preparing for shipment
- Order Packed: Item packed and ready to ship
- Order Shipped: Handed over to delivery partner
- Out for Delivery: Delivery person is on the way
- Delivered: Successfully delivered

=== HOW TO CANCEL AN ORDER ===

Before Shipment:
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Select the order you want to cancel
3. Click "Cancel Order"
4. Select cancellation reason
5. Click "Confirm Cancellation"
6. Refund initiated immediately

After Shipment:
You cannot cancel after shipping. Options:
1. Refuse delivery at your doorstep (inform delivery person)
2. Accept delivery and initiate return later

Auto-Cancellation:
Orders auto-cancelled if:
- Payment not completed within 24 hours
- Product goes out of stock
- Address unserviceable
- Suspected fraudulent activity

Refund Timeline:
- Original payment method: 7-10 business days
- DesiMart Wallet: Instant
- Bank transfer: 5-7 business days

=== HOW TO RETURN A PRODUCT ===

Eligibility Check:
- Product must be within 30-day return window
- Unused, in original packaging with all accessories
- Not in non-returnable category
- No damage or missing seal

Return Process:

1. Initiate Return Request
   - Go to "My Orders"
   - Select the item
   - Click "Return Item"

2. Select Reason
   - Received wrong product
   - Product damaged/defective
   - Product not as described
   - Changed mind
   - Found better price elsewhere
   - Other (specify)

3. Choose Refund Method
   - Original payment source (recommended)
   - DesiMart Wallet (instant credit)
   - Bank transfer

4. Schedule Pickup
   - Select convenient date and time slot
   - We'll arrange FREE pickup from your address
   - Ensure product is packed properly

5. Pickup Confirmation
   - Keep product ready with accessories, packaging
   - Delivery person will collect
   - You'll receive pickup confirmation

6. Quality Check
   - We inspect the product at our warehouse
   - Usually takes 2-3 business days

7. Refund Processing
   - If approved, refund initiated within 24 hours
   - DesiMart Wallet: Instant
   - Original method: 7-10 business days
   - Bank transfer: 5-7 business days

If Quality Check Fails:
- You'll be notified with reason
- Product will be returned to you

=== HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND ===

For Returns:
Follow return process (see above). Refund auto-initiated after quality check.

For Cancelled Orders:
Refund automatically processed when order is cancelled.

For Damaged/Defective Products:
1. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery
2. Share photos/videos of damage
3. We'll arrange pickup
4. Immediate replacement or refund (your choice)

For Wrong Product:
1. Contact support immediately
2. Send photos
3. We'll collect wrong item and send correct one
4. Or full refund if you prefer

Refund Status Check:
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Select the order
3. View "Refund Status"
4. Check refund tracking number

If Refund Delayed:
After 10 business days, if refund not received:
1. Email: refunds@desimart.com with:
   - Order number
   - Bank details
   - Screenshot of refund status
2. Call: 1800-123-4567
3. We'll escalate and resolve within 24 hours

=== HOW TO USE COUPON CODES ===

Finding Coupons:
- Check "Offers" page on website
- Email newsletters
- SMS updates
- App notifications
- Festival sale banners

Applying Coupons:
1. Add products to cart
2. Go to cart/checkout
3. Look for "Apply Coupon" box
4. Enter code (case-sensitive)
5. Click "Apply"
6. Discount will reflect in order total

Common Issues:
- "Coupon expired": Check validity date
- "Minimum order not met": Add more items
- "Not applicable on this product": Check product restrictions
- "Already used": Most coupons are one-time use

Stacking Coupons:
- Only 1 coupon per order
- Can combine with bank offers and cashback
- Sale prices + coupon = Allowed
- Multiple coupons on same order = Not allowed

2. Select "Saved Cards"
3. Add new card or delete old ones
4. CVV needed for verification

=== HOW TO CLAIM WARRANTY ===

For Manufacturing Defects:
1. Contact support: 1800-123-4567 or support@desimart.com
2. Provide:
   - Order number
   - Product details
   - Issue description
   - Photos/videos of problem
3. Our team will diagnose remotely
4. If repair/replacement needed:
   - We arrange free pickup
   - Send to service center
   - Repair/replace within 7-14 days
   - Free delivery back to you

For Extended Warranty:
If you purchased extended warranty:
1. Mention this while claiming
2. Better benefits (accidental damage, priority service)
3. Loaner device provided in some cases

What to Keep Ready:
- Original invoice/order receipt
- Warranty card (if any)
- Product with all accessories
- Original packaging (preferred)

Not Covered:
- Physical/liquid damage (unless extended warranty)
- Tampered serial numbers
- Unauthorized repairs
- Normal wear and tear

=== HOW TO UPDATE ACCOUNT DETAILS ===

Personal Information:
1. Go to "My Account"
2. Click "Profile Settings"
3. Edit name, email, phone
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Verify OTP (for email/phone changes)

Password Change:
1. Go to "Account Settings"
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (min 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Save

Manage Addresses:
1. Go to "My Addresses"
2. Add new address
3. Edit existing address
4. Delete old address
5. Set default address

Email Preferences:
1. Go to "Communication Preferences"
2. Choose what emails you want:
   - Order updates (cannot unsubscribe)
   - Promotional offers
   - New arrivals
   - Personalized recommendations
3. Save preferences

Delete Account:
1. "Account Settings" > "Delete Account"
2. Confirm deletion
3. Complete/cancel pending orders first
4. Wallet balance will be refunded
5. Cannot be undone
