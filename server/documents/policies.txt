DesiMart Policies and Terms

=== RETURN & REFUND POLICY ===

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on most products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Eligible Products for Return:
- Smartphones and tablets (if unused with original packaging)
- Laptops and computers (if unopened or with minimal usage)
- Cameras and photography equipment
- Smartwatches and wearables
- Gaming consoles (if unopened)
- Home appliances (if unused)

Non-Returnable Products:
- Products with tampered or missing serial numbers
- Products damaged due to misuse or negligence
- Opened software, games, or downloadable content
- Personal care items (earbuds, headphones with ear tips)
- Products on clearance or final sale

Return Process:
1. Go to "My Orders" in your account
2. Select the item you want to return
3. Choose "Return Item" and select reason
4. We'll arrange free pickup from your address
5. Refund will be processed within 7-10 business days after quality check

Refund Timeline:
- Original payment method: 7-10 business days
- DesiMart Wallet: Instant credit
- Bank transfer: 5-7 business days

Partial Refunds:
If product is returned in non-original condition, damaged, or with missing accessories, we may issue a partial refund (up to 30% deduction).

=== CANCELLATION POLICY ===

Before Shipment:
You can cancel any order before it's shipped without any charges. Go to "My Orders" and select "Cancel Order". Refund will be processed immediately.

After Shipment:
Once shipped, you cannot cancel the order directly. You must wait for delivery and initiate a return if needed. However, you can refuse delivery at your doorstep, and we'll process the refund after the product reaches our warehouse.

Auto-Cancellation:
Orders are automatically cancelled if:
- Payment fails after 3 attempts
- Delivery address is unserviceable
- Product goes out of stock after order placement
- Suspected fraudulent activity

Cancellation by DesiMart:
We reserve the right to cancel orders in cases of pricing errors, product unavailability, or suspected fraud. Full refund will be issued in such cases.
