CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE - SAMPLE DATA
==============================================

Refund Policy
-------------
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. To request a refund:
1. Contact our support team with your order number
2. Provide a brief reason for the refund request
3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
4. The refund will be credited to your original payment method

Please note: Digital products can only be refunded within 7 days if they have not been downloaded or accessed.

Shipping Information
--------------------
Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over $50)
Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15.99)
Overnight Shipping: 1 business day ($29.99)

International orders may take 10-21 business days depending on customs processing.

All orders are shipped from our warehouse in California. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Address Changes
---------------
You can update your shipping address up until your order has been shipped. To change your address:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Orders" section
3. Click on the active order
4. Select "Edit Shipping Address"

If your order has already shipped, please contact our support team immediately. We may be able to intercept the package or arrange for forwarding.

Account Management
------------------
Creating an Account:
- Visit our website and click "Sign Up"
- Enter your email and create a password
- Verify your email address
- Complete your profile

Password Reset:
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your registered email
- Check your email for reset instructions
- Reset link expires in 24 hours

Returns Process
---------------
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Items must be:
- Unused and in original condition
- In original packaging
- With all tags and labels attached

To initiate a return:
1. Contact support with your order number
2. We'll provide a prepaid return shipping label
3. Pack the item securely
4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location
5. Refund processed within 5-7 days of receiving the return

Exchange Policy
---------------
We offer exchanges for size, color, or product variants. The process is:
1. Request an exchange through your account
2. Select the item you want to exchange
3. Choose your preferred replacement
4. Ship back the original item using our prepaid label
5. New item ships once we receive the return

Warranty Information
--------------------
All electronic products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering:
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failures under normal use
- Software issues

Warranty does NOT cover:
- Accidental damage
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Normal wear and tear

To file a warranty claim:
1. Contact support with proof of purchase
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. We may request photos or videos of the problem
4. Approved claims receive free repair or replacement
